About Me

I am an aspiring software engineer from the bay area. I began my journey at UC Davis studying chemical engineering. After graduating and beginning work in Automation for a company called Zymergen, I quickly became passionate about software engineering. Realizing that my exposure to software development is only really about 25% of my job, I have made the decision to take the next step in my career and transition into software engineering.
